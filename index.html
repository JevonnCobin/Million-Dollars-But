How to Play the Game

A Million Dollars But is a game that puts your morals and imagination to the test, posing the question, "What would you do for a million dollars?" If you're ready to see how far you will go for a million dollars, scroll down below. pick yes or no to the scenarios then put down how many millions you've made.
